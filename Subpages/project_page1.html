I'm Andrii Tiazhkorob

My love for video games came from observing my father playing. When I was just a child every day I would wait for my father to come back from work to watch him play. Games gave me many things: from unforgettable experiences and emotions to knowledge and skills. Those things motivated me to become a game designer, I want to design experiences, share knowledge and teach people through games.

But I fully understand that dream alone is not enough to become a game designer. So, I was developing many different skills connected to understanding the game development process from 3D modeling and sound design to scripting. Still, I don't see an end to things I can learn, so I will continue to grow and evolve both as a personality and a designer.
