Mimic Sleuth

Mimic Sleuth is a mix of “Papers, Please” and “The Case of the Golden Idol” set in a fantasy world where everything revolves around storing stuff in chests. You will venture into dungeons and learn what differentiates a real chest from a mimic. Then, armed with a rulebook filled to the brim with chest knowledge, you will amass more gold than you can count.

2

Period:

Still going

Responsabilities:

Monster Mail

Monster Mail is a colorful, kid-friendly adventure where you deliver party invitations to monsters living in different areas of a vibrant island. Each monster has a small quest for you to complete before they’ll join the party in the main area. As you help them, the party slowly comes to life with decorations and characters that reflect each monster’s unique personality.

Team size:

5

Period:

2 month

Responsabilities:

Quest design, Gameplay design, Sound design, All round programing, Concepting, and Prototyping

Hands of My Hoard

Hands of My Hoard is an AR mobile game in which you play as a dragon defending its precious hoard of wealth from endless waves of human armies trying to take it for themselves. Immerse yourself in the role of a dragon by using a variety of inputs to activate powerful abilities, smash through countless enemies, become stronger with rare artifacts, and protect your hoard.

Team size:

6

Period:

3 weeks

Responsabilities:

Sound design, UI programing, Concepting, and Prototyping
